About AI Sorter

AI Sorter was founded with the mission to streamline document management for everyone and similar collaborative environments. Our platform leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help users upload, sort, and access files effortlessly — enhancing productivity and focus.

Our dedicated team combines expertise in AI and software development to deliver a secure, intuitive, and powerful tool that adapts to your unique needs. We believe that smart technology can free up time so consumers can concentrate on what truly matters — work and fun.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered solution for document and workflow management in international forums, empowering participants through innovation.
